We, as MEGANOIR, are a design studio based in India, comprising of a passionately dedicated all-stars team who make communication between businesses and their target audience effecient and effective through design. We are designers, not artists, who craft brands visually to fit the demand and sky-rocket sales figures. The design studio was found in 2021 and currently is working on multiple projects that cater to a large market demographic.
