GOOD BISCUITS


BRANDING | Media Production House


Good Biscuits is a production house based in Kolkata, India. The company specialises in brand photo and video shoots and comprises of our photography team. The company asked us to build the logo and visual identity for them.

We wanted to reflect the creative fluidity of the phrase "Good Biscuits" through the logo. Hence, the handwritten custom type follows the fluidity of liquid on a firm surface. The type is neatly bound together and the rising slant puts a youthful, energetic twist to the feel of the production company.

The doodles and artwork elements created for the company further underscores the playful, fluid, and creative energy that enriches the company's work ethics and business understanding.
