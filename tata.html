EasyCarBuy is an online car dealing company that enables to simplify the sales and purchases of cars in the Indian Market. Their aim is to provide the customers with an independent base to hunt for the latest cars on the site and suit their comfort to purchase from the website.

We were hired to shoot a car review for Tata Nexon EV, primarily showcasing its features and build. So our photography team decided to do the chief shoot around an abandoned airstrip with the EV in action, capturing its charisma throughout.
