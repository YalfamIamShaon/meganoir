SAAKHI


BRANDING & LOGO DESIGN | Clothing Brand


Saakhi is a new clothing brand that specialises in fashionable yet subtle ethnicwear. The owner of the company, Mrs. Soma Sarkar worked with us to build a visual identity along with the brandmark, business card and other assets. The brand caters to a large audience with an undisturbed focus on feminine clothing. Needless to say our end goal with the brand identity was to create something traditional yet modern. The brand identity perfectly embodies Saakhi woman, who embraces the past, strives in the present while looking up to the future.

The logomark centers itself around the flower symbol, which represents the inner semi-circle of the hindi letter "à¤–", the "kh" of the word "Saakhi". The bold, sans-serif custom type reinvents the notion of heritage and pride in traditional values and culture and packages the same with the notion of a new woman, situated in her roots and growing outward in a swift motion. The modern feel of the brandmark justly balances it's curves and corners to retain a hint of calligraphic mastery found in the puranas while appearing trendy. The brand identity system uses three different colours that reflects a spirit of festivity. The logomark and the flower symbol work across the brand asset as a decorational item as well, keeping the brand elements recognisable and close-knit.
