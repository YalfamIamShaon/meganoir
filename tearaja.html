TEA RAJA


CREATIVE SHOOT | Tea Brand


Tearaja is a tea brand that is rapidly changing the tea industry, unchanged for over two hundred years. Eliminating the slow supply chain and middle men, the tea service express delivers their products directly to their consumers. This process ensures the safety and low cost of their world-class selection of teas. The company assigned our photography team with the product shoot of their "Kolkata Edit" gift box.

The Tea Box contains an assortment of four exquisite tea which encapsulate the feeling that Kolkata, the City of Joy, brings to mind. Our photography team undertook the visual campaign of "Kolkata Edit" in November of 2020. The entire shoot was conducted in guirella mode, across the various landmarks of Kolkata The beauty of the products are utilised in an almost decorative fashion, enhancing the frames without disrupting their serenity. Landmarks such as the famous Howrah bridge and Ahiritola Ghaat are used keeping the product as the hero in multiple shots, creating a campaign that visually speaks to its audience.
