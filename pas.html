STERLIN


BRANDING | FINTECH


STERLING is a fintech brand that creates multi-layered solutions for simple, safe, and secure payments. The STERLING Speedster sub-brand aims to primarily cater people with supremely secure and elegant payment solution needs.

The logomark appears as the embodiment of the sense of robustness and swiftness that is attributed to the company by their consumer base, usually within the age bracket of 25 to 55 years. The word "Speedster", written in a lower case, slanted, and playful lettering contrasts the sturdy construction of the word "STERLING" and helps in modernizing and adding a casual element to the business centric brand.

The credit cards designed for the brand value practicality over aesthetics. The card number is laid out in a coloumn system for better legibility which also provides the design plenty of space. The yellow and lavender mix runs binds the identity system together. The employee ID is presented in a layout that highlights the most important data at the top. The identity system supports a brand that is confident about its banking operations and sustains elegant and swift payment methods.
